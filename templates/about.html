About TradeSphere

TradeSphere is a platform designed to streamline renewable energy trading. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to contribute to a sustainable future by trading renewable energy resources efficiently and effectively.

Built by a dedicated team of students with expertise in machine learning, backend development, and reporting, TradeSphere is the culmination of creativity, innovation, and teamwork.
